# Product Backlog

## Project
**SCARS — Sistema de Controle e Autonomia de Recursos Soberanos**

---

## Product Objective
Develop an analytical solution based on a Data Warehouse to consolidate data related to materials, technical hours, programs, and strategic projects, enabling historical and multidimensional cost analysis and supporting strategic decision-making.

---

## Business Value
The solution aims to reduce the manual effort required to consolidate information, improve the reliability of analytical data, and support strategic decision-making based on consolidated, traceable, and comparable indicators. In addition to cost visibility, the product is expected to provide budget monitoring, deviation analysis, financial health classification, and projected budget overrun insights.
